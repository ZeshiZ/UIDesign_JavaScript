Take a Vitual Hike Through Hundred Acre Woods

We are well aware that with the current pandemic going on, it may be hard to visit our forest so we have created this vitual hike for you to enjoy in the comfort and safety of your own home

Honeypot Trail

We called this the honeypot trail for a reason! As you walk along the trail you'll noticed the magnificent yellow path leading you through our forest. It is one of our favourite trails.

Piglet's Walk

When Piglet's need his alone time, this is the trail he take. As you go farther and farther into the trail, you'll see all the red mixed with yellow that reminds Piglet of his friendship with Winnie the Pooh.

Owl's Flight

When Owl needs to reflect on his own thoughts, this is the path he takes. THe highest and lows of this trail are no challenge for an owl who can fly.

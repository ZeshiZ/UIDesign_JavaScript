Before you hike

Watch this video to know basic skills before starting your first hike. Here you will see tips and tricks as well as equipments you will need to have an enjoyable and safe day hiking adventure.

What to bring:

  • Hiking backpack
  • Weather appropriate clothing
  • Hiking shoes or boots
  • Plenty of food
  • Plenty of water
  • Navigation tools such as a map and compass
  • First aid kit
  • Knife or multi-tool
